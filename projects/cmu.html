Augmented reality with planar homographies

Detected corresponding feature points between image pairs with BRIEF descriptors, computed the homography between them, and warped virtual content onto a real object — first overlaying a book cover onto a textbook, then extending it frame-by-frame to video.

BRIEF Homography AR
16-720 · Computer Vision

Lucas-Kanade tracking

Optical-flow tracking across video: a 2D translation tracker that handles template drift, dominant-motion estimation to find moving objects in aerial footage despite camera motion, and an inverse-compositional variant for efficiency. Tested on ants and vehicles in non-stationary footage.

Optical Flow Tracking Motion Estimation
16-720 · Computer Vision

Two-view 3D reconstruction

Recovered 3D structure from two viewpoints: matched features, estimated the fundamental matrix with the eight-point algorithm, derived the essential matrix from calibration, and triangulated correspondences into a point cloud — with automatic matching driven by epipolar constraints.

Eight-Point Epipolar Geometry Triangulation
16-720 · Computer Vision

Neural networks for character recognition

Handwritten-character recognition on NIST36. A from-scratch fully-connected network reached 76% test accuracy via morphological segmentation and bounding-box extraction; a PyTorch CNN improved this to 90.2% validation accuracy. A trained autoencoder for image compression reached an average PSNR of 15.68.

PyTorch CNN Autoencoders
16-720 · Computer Vision

Photometric stereo

Recovered shape from appearance under varying illumination. Calibrated photometric stereo extracted albedo and surface normals, integrated via Frankot-Chellappa into a depth map. The uncalibrated case estimated lighting with SVD and ran into the bas-relief ambiguity, where a family of depth configurations produce identical images.

Surface Normals SVD Bas-Relief Ambiguity

16-833 · SLAM

Robot localization with particle filters

Global localization for a lost indoor robot in Wean Hall using only odometry and a laser rangefinder against a known map. Implemented Monte Carlo localization — a non-parametric recursive Bayes filter — and validated it across three robot datasets.

Particle Filter Monte Carlo Localization LiDAR
